There once was a Boy, he loved computers. His life was a mystery, no one knew him. One day a mysterious QR-code appeared at the school. Everyone scanned, that brought them to a mysterious website, no one knew who made it or what it was for, it was a black site with a countdown.
The countdown was set to 48h from the day the Website was found. Everyone waited. On the day the countdown hit 0 there was a blackout. A Hacker infiltrated the whole world power grid. This hacker was powerful. He was the boy.
